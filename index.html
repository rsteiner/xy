About Us

XY Media Partners is a Los Angeles-based production company founded by a team of entertainment and technology professionals. Launched in the summer of 2024, our mission is to use technology to streamline the film and television production process while unlocking new channels for distribution revenue.

We operate with an eye for talent, a deep command of cutting-edge tools, and a commitment to production efficiency. By running multiple projects in parallel, we maximize both creative output and business value.

At XY, we believe technology should empower—not replace—human creativity. That's why we use AI to eliminate busywork, surface insights, and help our teams focus on what matters most: making exceptional content.

Aly Acosta is a seasoned filmmaker and performer with over 15 years of experience in front of and behind the camera. A Miami native, Aly got her start acting in high school theater before transitioning into film and television, where she has since built a diverse portfolio as a producer, director, and writer.

Aly has spearheaded a wide range of independent projects, developing a reputation for creative versatility and sharp storytelling instincts. Her work spans narrative shorts, indie features, and experimental formats—always grounded in a passion for character and emotional authenticity.

With a deep love for film—she's watched Ocean's Eleven over 100 times—Aly brings both a cinephile's heart and a producer's discipline to XY Media Partners, where she helps lead creative development and production execution across the slate.

William Barrantes

Partner, Production

William Joseph Barrantes Jr. is a seasoned professional in the entertainment industry, with a rich career spanning over two decades. William has made significant contributions to the fields of entertainment production and rights management.

William Joseph Barrantes Jr. is a seasoned professional in the entertainment industry, with a rich career spanning over two decades. William has made significant contributions to the fields of entertainment production and rights management.

William's career began with Ariztical Entertainment, and he has since held key positions at Ventura Distribution, Regent Entertainment, and Fabrication Films. At Fabrication Films, he served as the Director of Acquisitions, where he acquired independent full-length features and TV shows for international and domestic markets. He was directly responsible for marketing and releasing a library of over 100 titles, and he worked closely with international vendors to meet release deadlines.

In his role as a Sales Engineer at FilmTrack, Inc., William acted as a liaison between the company and potential clients, presenting the system to personnel worldwide. He trained new clients on system competency, script management, metadata management, and more. His work ensured that FilmTrack was professional and understandable to clients with varying levels of experience in rights management.

William's expertise in rights management was further honed during his tenure at Roku as a Product Manager for Rights & Content Tooling. He designed a rights management system for Roku's VOD platform, ensuring that all projects met their full financial potential. He collaborated with various stakeholders, including acquisitions, programming, original content, sales, operations, tech, engineering, IT, delivery, and finance.

William is a member of the Producers Guild of America West Coast and has produced numerous movies and TV episodes.

In his free time, William enjoys exploring new trends in the entertainment industry and staying updated with the latest advancements in multimedia distribution.
